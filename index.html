Privacy Policy

Rtrue Employee Attendance & Management Application

Effective Date: 01-01-2026

Riddhi Corporate Services Limited (RCSL, we, us, or our) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded when you use the Rtrue mobile application and related services.

Rtrue is an internal employee management and attendance application designed for organizational use. The application is used to manage employee attendance, shift scheduling, leave management, reward points, and role-based workflows. We are committed to protecting employee privacy and handling all information responsibly and securely.

Information We Collect

We collect basic employee information such as name, employee ID, phone number, email address, and assigned role (Supervisor or Operator). Attendance-related data including check-in and check-out records, shift schedules, rosters, leave information, and reward points are also collected for operational purposes.

Attendance and Work Activity

Attendance is recorded using QR code-based verification and supervisor approvals. Operators may accept shift invitations, after which a QR code is generated for attendance validation. Supervisors can approve attendance through QR scanning and may perform manual check-ins or check-outs when required.

Location Information

Location access is used only at the time of attendance actions to verify employee presence. The application does not track users continuously and does not collect location data in the background without user permission.

Camera Access

Camera permission is required only for scanning QR codes and optional face verification features. The application does not store photos, videos, or camera data on the device or servers.

Device Information

We collect limited device information such as device model, operating system version, and a unique device identifier. This information is used strictly for employee security, device verification, and prevention of unauthorized access.

Notifications

Notifications are used to inform employees about attendance updates, shift assignments, leave approvals, supervisor actions, and reward point updates. Notification access is optional and can be managed through device settings.

Account Creation

User accounts are not publicly created. All employee accounts are created and managed by the companys HR or administrative team. The application is intended for use within a single organization and is not a public consumer app.

Data Sharing

Employee data is not sold, shared, or distributed to third parties. Information is used solely for internal purposes such as payroll processing, employee performance evaluation, and workforce management.

Data Retention

Employee data is retained only for the duration of employment. Once an employee leaves the organization, their data is securely archived and no longer actively used.

Account Management

The application supports two types of accounts: Supervisor and Operator. While the attendance workflow differs between these roles, the handling of personal data remains the same for all users.

Childrens Privacy

This application is intended strictly for professional workplace use and is not designed for individuals under the age of 18.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Users will be informed of significant changes through official communication channels.
